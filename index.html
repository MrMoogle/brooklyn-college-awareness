About Us

Every Spring and Fall Break, we lead a weeklong community service trip to Brooklyn, New York. There, we work with high school students, many of whom are first generation college applicants or come from disadvantaged backgrounds. Our core mission is to help them achieve brighter futures by encouraging and inspiring them to continue their education beyond high school.

Frequently Asked Questions

I. What are you looking for in applicants?

We love enthusiastic, passionate applicants who we believe can strongly connect with the students that we work with. Prior experience with teaching and community service is also a huge plus.

II. How many applicants do you usually end up accepting?

For our fall trip, we usually take between 40% - 60% of those who apply. A lot less people apply in the spring so for our spring trip, we usually take between 90% - 100%. We give precedence to past trip participants and applicants who previously applied but did not get in. The bottom line is that if you really want to go, we'll find a way to eventually get you in .

III. How much is the trip fee?

Only $50! Yep, that's right. $50 will cover all food, accomodation, and transportation expenses for an entire week in New York City. Why such a low fee? We're extremely fortunate to be sponsored by Princeton ReachOut, an alumni organization dedicated to global community service. If are accepted and have difficulties paying the fee, let us know and we'll figure something out
